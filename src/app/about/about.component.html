My Python COVID-19 Twitter Bot

In this global pandemic, people often are curious of the current daily cases, deaths, and other general statistics of COVID-19. Due to this predicament, I have taken the opportunity to create a Twitter bot that will scrape data and automatically post COVID-19 statistics (as shown in the picture) to my Twitter Developer account @CovidNick. This script was written in Python and was pushed to Heroku in order to run 24/7, and posts every 24 hours at 6PM EST.
